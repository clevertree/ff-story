# Major Sections of the Book

This outline serves as the structural skeleton for **Forgotten Future**. We are focusing on conceptualizing these sections before diving into chapter-level writing.

## Act I: The Shaking (The Catalyst)
*Focus: The immediate aftermath of the Moon's destruction and the first Tripod landings.*
- **Setting:** Various Earth cities and the Lunar Colony remnants.
- **Key Event:** "The Moon shook and curled up like gentle fire."
- **Theme:** Chaos, disbelief, and the failure of current technology to respond.

## Act II: The Glazing (The Transformation)
*Focus: Long-term environmental collapse and the realization of the Apollo Trigger.*
- **Setting:** The Glass Oceans and the Smog-choked Megacities.
- **Key Event:** Discovery of the "Mechanical Cells" and the beginning of the Human-Tripod War.
- **Theme:** Adaptation, survival, and the loss of the biological self.

## Act III: The Great Beast (The Conflict)
*Focus: The peak of the war and the Nixon Revelation.*
- **Setting:** The Desert Base and the "Blinding Skies" above the front lines.
- **Key Event:** Retrieval of the unredacted Nixon transmission and the Siege of the Desert Base.
- **Theme:** Truth retrieval, resistance, and the scale of the cosmic threat.

## Act IV: Hyper Sleep (The Conclusion)
*Focus: The final gamble for humanity's future.*
- **Setting:** The Pillars of Fire and the Orbital Fragments.
- **Key Event:** The transition to Hyper Sleep as the Earth becomes uninhabitable.
- **Theme:** Hope in stasis, transcendence, and the "Forgotten Future."
