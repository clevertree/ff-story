# Chapter 62: The Peaceful Emissary

## Outline / Summary
- **The Diplomatic Gambit:** The Core sends a massive emissary to Arlo's village. They arrive without technology, making a show of respect and "plurality."
- **The Offer:** They identify themselves as Core representatives and claim they have changed. They offer to leave the village intact forever and make their classification official, provided Arlo comes peacefully to the desert base.
- **The Threat:** Arlo recognizes the subtext: "Come with us or we destroy your village."
- **The Refusal:** Arlo refuses to leave, stating that his place is with his people.

## Character Development
- **Arlo:** Shows remarkable stoicism and wisdom. He refuses to be moved by the Core's performance of "peace."
- **Core Representatives:** Polished, respectful, but ultimately representing an ultimatum.

## POV & Focus
- **POV:** Arlo.
- **Focus:** The tension between the performative diplomacy and the underlying threat of violence.

## Timing
- Year 22 AT.

## World-Building Integration
- **Classification Status:** The Core's bureaucratic method of "allowing" certain analog zones to exist while maintaining overall control.
- **Technology-Free Protocols:** A rare instance of the Core operating without their usual artificial enhancements to win trust.

## Notes & Questions
- Arlo is referred to as Arlo, but the Core treats him as the vessel for the "Maya" spirit.
