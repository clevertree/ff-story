# Chapter 73: The Aetheric Wake

**Objective:** Lem's transition to the dreamscape and Maya's resurrection through the Inception Procedure.

**Key Events:**
- Lem transitions to the "clearing" dreamscape after the orbital strike, finding stability through the physical shards of his Mission 3 Wood-Vessel.
- AI Lynn uses a high-limit beacon scan (Core and Analog resonance) to track aetheric soul signatures.
- AI Lynn initiates the "Inception Procedure" to guide Arlo's spirit away from the Core's restorative traps.
- AI Lynn and Arlo's spirit merge, resulting in the re-emergence of **Maya**, who regains her 202X identity.
- Maya researches the global history of the Core's betrayal; she grows increasingly enraged by the "Thousand-Year Fallacy."
- Maya remembers Arlo's recent village memories, fueling her rage before she departs Lem's dreamscape to hunt the Core.
- Lem follows Maya's signature into the "warm path" of the Cradle light to protect her.
