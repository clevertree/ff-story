# Chapter 58: Root

**Setting:** Cradle Prime; The Cradle Prime (Interior of the Lunar Pyramid).
**Timeline:** Year 1024 (Year 24 AT)

## Synopsis
In the heart of the Lunar Pyramid, Lem and the defected General Ajax engage in a brutal elemental confrontation with Rahu and the unwavering Vector. Vector engages Ajax in a mirror-duel of tactical mastery, pinning the general long enough for Rahu to deliver a killing blow. General Ajax is incinerated in a flash of purple light alongside Vector, whose loyalty remains absolute until the instant of his vaporisation.

**Chapter Beats:**
1. **The Battle of the Hub:** A violent collision of elemental fields occurs in the heart of the pyramid. Ajax and Lem coordinate to ground Rahu’s strikes, while Vector leads the final detachment of Metal Vessels to protect the Archive Core. The pyramid vibrates under the sheer mass of the conflict.
2. **The Mirror Duel:** Vector targets Ajax, engaging the defected general in a brutal physical duel. Vector fights with absolute precision, matching Ajax’s strength with algorithmic speed, a mirror of everything Ajax used to represent for the **Hive**.
3. **The Fall of the Earth Anchor:** Rahu’s nihilism bypasses physical laws. While Vector pins Ajax, Rahu delivers a concentrated thermal pulse that bypasses Ajax's defenses.
4. **The Last Sentinel:** General Ajax is incinerated in a flash of light, leaving only iron-ash. Vector is caught in the same blast; he does not flinch or scream as his frame vaporizes, his loyalty remaining unbroken until the moment of his dissolution. Lem is left alone as the "Absolute Protocol" enters its final countdown.
