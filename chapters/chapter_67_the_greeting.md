# Chapter 67: The Greeting

## Outline / Summary
- **Entering the Spire:** Lem enters the main apparatus of the Spire City, moving toward the central hub.
- **The First Contact:** A Gorgon in a small, six-legged vehicle approaches Lem. It carries no visible weapons and shows no aggression.
- **The Mutual Greeting:** Lem initiates a peaceful gesture by raising his arm. The Gorgon mirror the action, raising its own arm in greeting.
- **Silent Understanding:** The recognition that the Gorgons are not merely automated tools but intelligent entities capable of communication and caution.

## Character Development
- **Lem:** Approaches the "enemy" with respect rather than dominance.
- **The Gorgon Liaison:** Represents the first peaceful, individual interaction with a "wild" Synodic entity since the exile.

## POV & Focus
- **POV:** Lem.
- **Focus:** The tension of the first meeting and the bridge of communication.

## Timing
- Year 22 AT.

## World-Building Integration
- **Gorgon Vehicles:** Introduction of specialized Synodic transport for individual entities.
- **Non-Aggression Protocols:** The discovery that "wild" Gorgons follow a defensive, observant strategy rather than overt hostility.

## Notes & Questions
- The Gorgons know not to take the first action; they are watching the humans to see their intent.
