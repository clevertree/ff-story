# Chapter 49: The Peaceful Emissary

## Synopsis
The Core's diplomatic gambit. A respectful emissary arrives with an ultimatum, while Cassia Vane sends a secret plea to Arlo revealing "Plan B."

## Key Beats
- The arrival of the "Peaceful Emissary."
- The terms of the ultimatum: total synchronization or destruction.
- Cassia Vane's clandestine message; the cracks in the Core's leadership.
- The tension in the village as they weigh the offer.
