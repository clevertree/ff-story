## Synopsis
Briefing on the "Utopian Cities" project and the need for orbital coordination.


---

## Draft
The briefing focused on technical solutions as they explained that the Dead Zone needed to be cleared for the Hive's expansion. Watching the patterns of surveillance on every route, I noted how sensors tracked the movement of every citizen.

Moving in a large group, we deployed the Buoy, causing hostile machines in the area to malfunction from signal interference. Monoliths fired projectiles while the area filled with the smell of ozone and fire.

Following the destruction of my vehicle and a subsequent reset, a unit failure occurred before my system returned and I woke in an incubation chamber. Once our units were repaired and sent back to the field, I watched the city construction continue as citizens began their assigned tasks.

They used the term 'hero' in their reports, the utility of which I understood for encouraging the other workers.

## Character Arc
**Lem's Desensitization:**
- Traumatized then numbed by the "immortality" protocol.
- Death becomes a technical glitch rather than an end.
- He begins to see the Metal Vessels as brothers in servitude, noting they look to him for leadership.

**The "Hero" Burden:**
- Forced to play the role of the savior for a civilian population that doesn't understand the cost.
- His recognition as a leader (New Rahu) is encouraged by the Core to cement his loyalty.

## World-Building Integration
✅ **Facts Applied:**
- The Equatorial Dead Zone: A high-Aether, high-heat environment.
- Aetheric Buoys: Create a "Disruption Net" to clear wild Synodics.
