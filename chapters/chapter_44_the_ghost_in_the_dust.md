# Chapter 44: The Ghost in the Dust

## Synopsis
The Crew's refusal to quit leads to a direct assault on the main base. This chapter combines the mobilization and the symmetric warfare in the desert plain as Moon pods launch a counter-attack.

## Key Beats
- Massive mobilization of the hybrid wood-core fleet.
- The desert plain becomes a battlefield of "Falling Suns."
- The realization that the Core is reacting with symmetric force.
- The psychological impact of seeing the "Perfect Hero" (Lem) leading a rebellion.
