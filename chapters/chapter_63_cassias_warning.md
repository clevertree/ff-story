# Chapter 63: Cassia's Warning

## Outline / Summary
- **Cassia's Intervention:** Cassia Vane, now deeply concerned for the future and attempting to broker a true peace, seeks a one-on-one conversation with Arlo.
- **The Secret Warning:** She admits the Core's past mistakes and warns that "Plan B" (total destruction of the village) is the inevitable next step if Arlo doesn't evacuate or cooperate.
- **The Plea:** Cassia tries to reason with Arlo, asking him to save his people by leaving with the emissary.
- **The Lost Interest:** Most Core ambassadors have already lost interest in the negotiation, leaving Cassia as the only one still trying to prevent the catastrophe.

## Character Development
- **Cassia Vane:** Emerges as a tragic mediator, trying to prevent a massacre she knows her own organization is capable of.
- **Arlo:** Remains unmoved by the fear of the "cost."

## POV & Focus
- **POV:** Cassia Vane.
- **Focus:** The desperation of a moderate caught between a peaceful village and a ruthless technocracy.

## Timing
- Year 22 AT (Immediately following Chapter 62).
