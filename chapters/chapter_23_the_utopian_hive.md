# Chapter 23: The Utopian Hive

## Synopsis
The Hive City takes shape as the perimeter is cleared. Lem is awarded as the "New Hero," but quickly realizes that the paradise being built is a prison for the human spirit.

## Key Beats
- Perimeter cleared; celebratory broadcast for the "Perfect Hero."
- Construction of the Hive infrastructure; the efficiency is terrifying.
- Lem watches as the first "citizens" are processed.
- The realization that "Security" and "Liberty" are being redefined by the Core.
