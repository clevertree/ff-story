# Chapter 31: Stasis and Awakening

## Synopsis
Years pass in electrostatic stasis. Lem experiences recursive visions of the White Forest massacre before finally returning to reality in the clearing.

## Key Beats
- The frozen subjective time of stasis.
- The recursive "Ghost" loop; Lem's mind processing the trauma.
- Transition to reality; the forest has changed, reclaimed by nature.
- The first physical movement in a new, unrecorded era.
