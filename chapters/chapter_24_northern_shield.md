# Chapter 24: Northern Shield

## Synopsis
Lem is redeployed to the North. This chapter combines the Mission 3 briefing with the rapid deployment to the village borders. Lem meets Arlo for the first time in a tactical capacity.

## Key Beats
- Briefing on the "uncooperative" northern villages.
- Rapid deployment; the contrast between the Hive and the frozen north.
- Lem observe the villagers' distrust of technology.
- Initial scouting of the village perimeter; Lem's first glimpse of Arlo's leadership.
